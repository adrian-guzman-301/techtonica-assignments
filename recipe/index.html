Peanut Butter Pickle Tuna Smoothie

This salty, soury, fishy, creamy, vinegary delight hits all the wrong sensory notes at once and is well-balanced enough that your body can technically ingest it.

Photo Credit: Google AI Studio

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna (in water or oil, drained)
  • 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup dill pickle juice (plus a few chopped pickles)
  • 1/2 cup milk (or non-dairy alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Ice cubes (optional, for texture)

Directions

  1. Dump everything into the blender.
  2. Blend until smoothness preference reached.
  3. Pour into a tall glass.
  4. Garnish with a pickle spear.
  5. Drink and gag.
