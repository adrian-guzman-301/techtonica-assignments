dragonslayer

You are a powerful knight in the heat of battle against a dragon!
You've been fighting for days but can still manage to swing your weapon.
Your battle fatigue and the dragon's agility means you can only successfully
hit the dragon 50% of the time with each swing.

If you hit the dragon 4 times by 10th swing, you slay the dragon!

If you don't hit the dragon 4 times by 10th swing, the dragon will slay you!
