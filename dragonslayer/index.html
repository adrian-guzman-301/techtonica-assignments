dragonslayer

You are a powerful knight in the heat of battle against a dragon!
You've been fighting for days but can still manage to swing your weapon.

Your battle fatigue and the dragon's relentless agility means your
swings only hit 50% of the time.

If you hit the dragon 4 times by 10th swing, you slay the dragon!

If you don't hit the dragon 4 times by 10th swing, the dragon slays you!

Each successful hit reduces the dragon's remaining health by 25%.
